Video Sharing

To invite others to watch, the camera can be shared through the device sharing function in the settings menu of the camera’s plugin in the Mi Home/Xiaomi Home app. The person the camera is shared with needs to download the Mi Home/Xiaomi Home app and sign in with a Mi account.
