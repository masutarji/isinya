Real-time Surveillance
The real-time feed of the camera can be viewed in the camera’s plugin in the Mi Home/Xiaomi Home app, and the resolution and horizontal angle of the camera can be adjusted. Furthermore, you can make screenshots of or record the real-time footage, and make remote calls.

Infrared Night Vision
The camera can automatically switch to night vision mode at night according to the degree of change in the surrounding light. The built-in infrared night vision light has a night vision distance of up to 9 meters and can even shoot clearly in the dark.
